Lasagna

An Italian cassarole made with pasta, cheese, and sauce.

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

  • Italian sausage
  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Crushed garlic
  • Crushed tomato
  • Tomato paste
  • Tomato sauce
  • Water
  • Pinch of sugar
  • Basil leaves
  • Italian seasoning
  • Pepper
  • Parsley
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Mozzerella cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  1. In pot cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic on medium heat until brown, then stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water.Season with Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and basil, fennel, and parsley, then simmer for about an hour and a half
  2. Bring large pot of salted water to a boil to cook the noodles, then add noodles to boiling water for 8-10 minutes.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and parsely in bowl.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F, or 90 degrees C
  5. Assemble by spreading meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan, place noodles over meet sauce, then layer ricotta mixture over noodles, then top with mozzerella slices, then repeat until pan is full, or you run out of ingredients. After assembly top with generous amounts of parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake in over for 25 minutes civered by foil. Remove the foil and bake again for 25 minutes.
