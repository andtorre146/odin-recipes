Chicken Chili

A bowl of creamy chicken soup with some spices

Chicken Chili is a heavy comfort food, definitely not a low calorie alternative to regular chili, but well worth the amount of warmth this dish can provide. It's pretty close to common beef chili but white and creamy instead of red and spicy, and most importantly, can be made in a single stockpot for the people who don't want a huge mess!

  • Chicken
  • Chicken Broth
  • White Beans
  • Cilantro
  • Green Chiles
  • Cream Cheese
  • Cumin
  • Dried Oregano
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Chili Powder
  1. Cook onions and garlic in large pot.
  2. Add in chicken, chicken broth, beans, chiles, and spices. Boil and reduce heat, and simmer.
  3. Simmer for 30-40 minutes, without a lid, or until it has a consistency that you enjoy.
  4. Turn off heat and slowly stir in heavy cream, and cream cheese.
  5. Serve chili into bowls and top with desired ingredients
